About

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Sapienza University of Rome, Italy. My research focuses on multi-paradigm process models for discovery, monitoring and reasoning, with a particular interest in the Object-Centric Event Data (OCED) meta-model and its semantic encoding.

My broader research interests include formal methods, smart cities, Internet of Things (IoT), and formal modeling of complex systems. I have worked extensively with specification and verification techniques such as TLA+ and VDM-SL, and with formal models for smart city scenarios including smart markets, parking, waste management, traffic monitoring, and cyber-physical systems.

Before starting my Ph.D., I held academic and teaching positions in Pakistan as a Research Associate at COMSATS University Islamabad, Sahiwal campus, a Web Instructor at Desired Technologies software house, and a Lecturer at Govt Binat ul Islam College, Sahiwal. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
